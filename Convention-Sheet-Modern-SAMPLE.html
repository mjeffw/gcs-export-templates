Notes
Aldric Voss is a landless knight who travels the realm seeking glory and righting wrongs. He was trained at the Academy of the Silver Blade and still wears their sigil—a silver sword on a blue field. His horse, Stormwind, is stabled at the inn (not carried equipment).
Combat notes: With Combat Reflexes he never freezes, and gets +1 to all active defenses and +6 to Fright Checks. Weapon Master (Broadsword) adds +2 per die to damage with his primary weapon.
